Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025
Effective date: October 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gwadar Complaint Helper ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name: To identify complaint submitters
  • Phone Number: For communication regarding complaints
  • Address: To locate and resolve issues
  • Email Address: For updates and notifications (optional)

2.2 Complaint Information

  • Complaint Type: Sanitation, drainage, or other community issues
  • Description: Details about the reported problem
  • Photos: Images related to the complaint (optional)
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for accurate problem mapping

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system
  • App Usage: Features used, time spent in app
  • Log Data: Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process Complaints: Review, track, and resolve submitted issues
  • Communication: Contact you regarding complaint status updates
  • Service Improvement: Analyze data to enhance community services
  • Location Services: Map complaints for efficient resolution
  • Support: Provide customer support and technical assistance

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Local Authorities: Relevant departments for complaint resolution
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Data is securely stored using Supabase cloud services
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Backup: Regular backups to prevent data loss

6. Data Retention

  • Active Complaints: Retained until resolution and follow-up period
  • Resolved Complaints: Kept for 2 years for reference and analysis
  • Personal Data: Deleted upon user request (subject to legal requirements)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of data collection (may limit app functionality)

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Location Services

Our app may request access to your device's location services to:

  • Automatically fill location information for complaints
  • Provide accurate mapping of reported issues
  • Improve service delivery efficiency

You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit some app functionality.

10. Camera and Photo Access

Our app may request access to your camera and photo gallery to:

  • Allow you to attach photos to complaint reports
  • Provide visual evidence of reported issues

Photo sharing is optional and you can submit complaints without images.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending notifications through the app (for significant changes)
